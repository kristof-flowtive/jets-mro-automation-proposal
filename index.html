The Approach

Proposed Solution

Two connected systems. The first watches a structured Google Drive folder, analyzes every new asset with GPT-4o vision, and produces one platform-tailored post per day across Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube. Captions are generated by a brand voice model trained on your existing approved posts, then routed to an Airtable approval board before scheduling through Metricool.

The second monitors your LinkedIn company page, drafts response suggestions in your voice for every comment and recruiting inquiry, and routes them to a one-click approval queue in Slack. Common, low-risk replies can auto-send once the voice is dialed in. Anything sensitive or recruiting-related stays human-first.

The single biggest risk you flagged is generic AI voice. That is exactly what the brand voice training phase and approval loop are built to solve. The system improves every week by learning from your edits.
